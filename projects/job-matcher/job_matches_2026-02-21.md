## Patterns & Observations

1. **Jooble was the MVP.** It returned the only score 8-9 matches (actual translator jobs in Minneapolis). RemoteOK and Remotive were heavily tech-skewed.

2. **The bilingual + content + photography combo is a genuine differentiator** for marketing roles at companies serving diverse or Latin American markets (see the Walter listing at score 8).

3. **"Traveling with Tasha"** has 4-5 similar content/marketing roles posted across the Twin Cities metro — worth reaching out directly rather than applying to each individually.

4. **Translation-specific jobs** are scarce on general job boards. For more translation work, consider registering directly on ProZ.com and TranslatorsCafe.

5. **Photography** roles are mostly freelance/gig (wedding, street) rather than full-time. The most viable photography path is pairing freelance shoots with a content or marketing day job.

6. **Many "bilingual" jobs** in the 300-350 range were customer service/call center roles where Spanish is just for talking to customers — not professional translation. These scored 1-4 and were excluded.

7. **Salary range** across matches: $18/hr (CSR fallback) to $163k (GM stretch). The sweet spot for realistic matches is $45,000-$75,000 for full-time content/marketing roles, or $25-$50/hr for freelance translation/photography.
