## Abstract

Two-study paper examining differential effects of avoidance vs. impression management safety behavior subtypes. Study 1 cross-sectional (n=487 online). Study 2 experimental dyadic design (n=96 undergraduates). Used Social Behaviour Questionnaire (SBQ) to assess subtypes.
