## Abstract

Not yet retrieved. Known from citations: Meta-analytic review of safety behaviors in exposure therapy. N=23 studies reviewed. Aggregate finding described as "inconclusive and could not provide strong evidence supporting either the removal or addition of safety behaviors during exposure" (cited in Blakey & Abramowitz 2016).
