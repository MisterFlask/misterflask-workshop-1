## Abstract

Brief 5-session transdiagnostic treatment (F-SET) focusing on identification and elimination of "false safety behaviors." Randomized controlled trial comparing F-SET to waitlist (N=28). Significant reductions in anxiety (d=2.01) and depression (d=2.16). Change in avoidance strategies mediated symptom change.
