## Abstract

Not yet retrieved. Known from Blakey & Abramowitz (2016) Table 2 summary: Study examined differential effects of avoidance vs. impression management SB subtypes in social anxiety disorder (n=93). Found SBs interfered with outcomes. SAD-specific sample with idiosyncratic SB identification.
