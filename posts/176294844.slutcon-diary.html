Takeaways

Boundaries

I’m thinking a lot about boundaries and how I feel about accidentally violating them in retrospect. I did the hand-on-throat-thing with Magpie without asking. I misread a “maybe” with Elspeth and got immediate feedback about same. I kept up arousal-based teasing with Cinder after hearing an explicit boundary of “no sex”, and she told me the next day that when I did so it made her feel like she was being a bad person. I’m glad she told me; I didn’t figure it might be taken that way.

My immediate gut-level reaction to all of these revelations was “wow, I sure burned that bridge” and that appears to generally not have been true. Empirically, a lot of what I learned is that light accidental boundary violations aren’t the end of the world if you try to avoid them, learn from your mistakes, and do your best to keep attuned to the other person’s well-being.

Frankly I still feel on a gut-level like writing all these missteps down is hugely risky in some way.
